Who We Are

Central Jersey Student Innovators' Association (CJSIA) is comprised of individuals from around New Jersey that share a strong passion for entrepreneurship, leadership, and innovation. Integral to our identity is the belief that innovation and risk are a vital contributor to societal progress. This is why since its creation in 2014, CJSIA has helped students build leadership/critical thinking skills, connected budding entrepreneurs to leaders in industry, and instilled a mindset of creativity/ambition within its members.

Our Mission

CJSIA’s founding goal is to provide an outlet for students to share and hone their skills in innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurship. We believe that in each student lies the potential to change the world in some way, shape, or form. The Central Jersey Student Innovators' Association is here to help realize this potential and transform ideas into plans primed for execution in the real world.
